FAQ

Common home lending questions.

Is Home Lending Advisor a lender?

No. This site is positioned as an advisory resource. It does not issue loans, make credit decisions, or guarantee loan terms.

Can this replace professional financial or legal advice?

No. Use this guidance as general education and preparation. Speak with licensed mortgage, legal, tax, or financial professionals before making a final decision.

What should I prepare before asking for guidance?

Bring your goal, estimated purchase price or current loan balance, timeline, credit range, and any loan estimates you are comparing.
